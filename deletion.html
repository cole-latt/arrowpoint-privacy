Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your ArrowPoint account and what data gets removed

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your ArrowPoint account directly from the mobile app:

  1. Open the ArrowPoint app on your device
  2. Navigate to your Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon in top right)
  4. Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section
  5. Tap "Delete Account"
  6. Read the confirmation warning carefully
  7. Tap "Delete Account" again to confirm
  8. Enter your password to verify
  9. Tap "Confirm Deletion" to complete the process

Your account will be deleted immediately, and you will be logged out of the app.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Profile Information: Username, email address, full name, bio, location, avatar image
  • Vault Items: All your saved arrowhead finds, photos, descriptions, and metadata
  • Frames: All custom frames and collections you created
  • Social Content: All your posts, comments, likes, and votes
  • Social Connections: Your followers, following list, and blocked users
  • Notifications: All notification history
  • Settings: All app preferences and customizations
  • Usage Data: Smart ID history, Manual ID searches, and usage statistics
  • Analytics: App usage patterns and behavioral data

Data Retention Period

Immediate Deletion: Your account is deactivated immediately and you can no longer log in.

30-Day Grace Period: Your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. This includes:

  • All database records
  • All uploaded images from cloud storage
  • All cached data
  • All backup copies

üìã Legal Retention Note: Some anonymized data may be retained for up to 90 days for legal compliance, security investigations, or fraud prevention. This data cannot be linked back to you personally.

What Happens to Your Content

Public Posts: Any posts you shared publicly will be deleted. Other users will see "[deleted]" in place of your username if they commented on your posts.

Comments: Your comments on other users' posts will be deleted.

Likes & Votes: All your likes and votes will be removed, and vote counts will be updated accordingly.

Data Protection Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

If you're located in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional data rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we use your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@arrowpoint.app

What Happens After Deletion

Immediate Effects:

  • You will be logged out of all devices
  • Your profile will disappear from search and discovery
  • Your username becomes available for others to use after 90 days
  • You cannot log in or recover your account

Creating a New Account:

  • You can create a new account with the same email after deletion is complete (30 days)
  • Your old data will NOT be restored to the new account
  • You will start fresh with no previous data or connections
